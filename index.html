The photographer

Oussama — surfer, filmmaker, and wave storyteller.

From Safi, shaped by salt and wind, Oussama learned the ocean as a language. He doesn’t just shoot surfing — he captures what it feels like: the breath before the paddle, the silence in the lineup, and the seconds that become memories.

Our approach

Every set is a story — light, timing, and the people inside the wave.

From Safi, shaped by salt and wind, Oussama and Ali are brothers by Ocean and by soul. Raised where the waves of Essaouiria and Safi Beach and Rass Lefaa carve stories into stone, they learned early that the ocean is not a destination but a language.

What began at home became a shared vision brought to life through movement, trust, and time spent chasing the horizon together. Rooted in Safi and carried outward, their journey now flows across Europe, from the raw Atlantic energy to the soft light of Tuscany.

They are wave storytellers, traveling with intention, turning fleeting moments into lasting memories. Guided by water, instinct, and connection, they do not simply ride waves but preserve the feeling they leave behind.

Built by Oussama & Ali — photographers and surfers from Safi أ 54 — Lefaa exists to turn sessions into memories you can keep.

“Strong images come from strong rhythm: the ocean sets the pace — we frame it.”
