Our approach

Every set is a story — light, timing, and the people inside the wave.

We don’t just take photos — we chase moments. The calm before the paddle, the tension in the lineup, the release inside the barrel. Pass Leaf is a rare kind of wave, and Safi is a meeting point: locals and travelers, all here for the same ocean rhythm. That’s the story we frame.

Built by Oussama & Ali — photographers and surfers from Safi — Lefaa exists to turn sessions into memories you can keep.

“Strong images come from strong rhythm: the ocean sets the pace — we frame it.”
